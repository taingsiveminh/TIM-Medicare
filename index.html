About

TIM Medicare is an independent distributor of medicines, medical devices and consumables, and haemodialysis solution that is focusing on hospitals, retail pharmacies, and health care clinics in the Kingdom of Cambodia. Established in 2020 plus several years experience with this medical industry.

To provide high quality, affordable pharmaceutical products and devices to healthcare providers and pharmacies, ensuring timely delivery and a reliable supplier. To become a leading distributor of pharmaceutical products and devices known for quality service, integrity, and customer satisfaction.
